How do I get started with the Growth App?

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for a free account, and you'll be guided through the setup process. You can start using the app right away without any credit card required.

What features are included in the free plan?

The free plan includes real-time monitoring, task management, appointment scheduling, and basic analytics. You can manage your contacts and sales pipeline effectively with these features.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. Simply go to your account settings and choose the Pro plan to access advanced features and priority support.

Is there a free trial for the Pro plan?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for the Pro plan. You can explore all the advanced features without any credit card required during the trial period.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via email or live chat. We are here to help you with any questions or issues you may have.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term commitments, and you can downgrade to the free plan whenever you choose.
